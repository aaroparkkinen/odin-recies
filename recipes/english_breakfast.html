English Breakfast

Delicious full english breakfast for one

English Breakfast is a cutural wonder of UK which is probably one of the most nutritious meal a human can have. Since the The Middle Ages the leaders of the world have cherished it and for a reason.

It contains 97% of your daily vitamin B intake and various other minerals for the well-being. For it's made in 10 minutes, it is the perfect meal to start your day off.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Beans
  • Shrooms
  • tomatoes
  • coffee

Cooking

  1. Start brewing your coffee and put the hob on
  2. Cut the tomatoes and mushrooms as you like them
  3. With nice olive oli or butter, start frying your ingredients
  4. Enjoy the beautiful meal and the birds singing outside your window
