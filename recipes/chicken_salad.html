Chicken salad with grapes in a red bowl

Chicken salad is a super versitile, healthy and easy to prepare meal. It dominates the lunchtime options in a office envirnoment, since it's meant to be eaten cold. With different ingredients and fresh sauces it never gets boring and should be a staple in everyone's weekly meal rotation.

Containing so many antioxidants, vitamins and minerals, it keeps people energetic and filled afterwards. The only downside is that being full of various premium ingredients, the bill gets expensive quite easily.

How to pick the best ingredients for chicken salad?

  • Check the dates, you don't want to eat outdated veges.
  • Focus on the quality of the sauce, chicken and tomatoes. Trust me!
  • Pick a salad sort that you like, most of the are actually almost the same in terms of nutritional contents (acc. Swedish study)

Preparation

  1. Cut the vegetables, onions and chicken into nice pieces
  2. fry the chicken
  3. add olive oil and any other sauce you like, keep in mind the calorie intake
  4. put the masterpiece together and enjoy!
